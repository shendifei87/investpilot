What is InvestPilot?

InvestPilot is a deep fundamental investment research framework built on Claude Code. It emulates the workflow of a buy-side equity research analyst at a fundamental-driven hedge fund — systematically identifying significantly undervalued stocks with high reward-to-risk and high probability of materialization within 0–3 months.

Rather than surface-level screening, InvestPilot performs a rigorous, evidence-based 9-step process: from business deep-dive and competitive moat analysis through financial modeling, Monte Carlo simulation, and risk-reward strategy — culminating in a research director review.

Investment Philosophy

🔍

Expectation Gap

Alpha comes from identifying gaps between market consensus and reality — not from following the crowd.

🧮

Self-Calculated Valuation

Every PE, PB, EV/EBITDA is computed from raw financial data. No pre-computed numbers from news or reports.

🎲

Probabilistic Thinking

Monte Carlo simulations with t-Copula dependency structures. Distributions, not point estimates.

⚖️

Contrarian Discipline

Every step ends with a mandatory contrarian check. "What if I'm wrong?" is not optional.

⏳

Time Decay Aware

Edges decay. Catalysts expire. Position sizing adjusts. Kelly half with conviction modifier.

🌐

Multi-Market

A-shares, Hong Kong, and US equity markets — each with purpose-built data pipelines.
